Race phases
First 500 m
Do not chase the fastest starters. They should feel: "This is fast, but controlled."
Middle 1.5 km
This is where the race becomes uncomfortable. Tell them: "Relax your face, strong arms, catch one person at a time."
Last 800 m
Start pushing. Tell them: "Now the race begins."
Last 300 m
Use everything left. Tell them: "Fast arms, quick feet, run through the finish line."
Race Script + Final Tips 14 / 14

Simple Race Script

Give them this before the start
"Start controlled. Don't panic. Find rhythm. Stay strong in the middle. With 800 m to go, start racing. With 300 m to go, give everything."
The three sessions that mattered most
  • Wednesday 3 June: 4 × 400 m + 4 × 200 m
  • Friday 5 June: 3 × 800 m
  • Monday 8 June: 2 × 400 m + 3 × 200 m

Everything else is easy.

Final reminder

Do not try to squeeze in more fitness now. In the last week, freshness beats extra training.
